Adult Group Lessons

Join us for group lessons - both members and non-members are welcome. Group training sessions are designed to improve stroke technique, broaden shot selection and increase your overall enjoyment of tennis. Use the NTRP guidelines to initially select the appropriate level for you - coaches will group players according to skill level after the first lesson.

Group lessons provide you with the opportunity to meet other likeminded players at your level. Practice is important between lessons to maximize the value of each lesson received. If you would like to practice with one of your fellow attendees, then let them know of your interest.

Registration for all programs is done online.
